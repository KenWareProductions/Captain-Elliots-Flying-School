Our Mission

Driving change through drone technology.

Our Vision

At Captain Elliot's Flying School, our mission is to harness the power of drones to address global challenges, empower communities, and protect our planet. We aim to be a leader in using innovative technology for charitable causes, ensuring that every flight makes a difference.

Key Goals

  • Provide humanitarian aid by delivering essential supplies to remote and disaster-affected areas.
  • Support environmental conservation through wildlife monitoring and habitat protection.
  • Educate and inspire the next generation through drone training and STEM programs.
  • Promote safe drone use by teaching the UK Drone Code and best practices.

Our Impact

Since our inception, we’ve delivered over 10,000 medical kits, trained 5,000 students in drone operation, and monitored 100+ wildlife habitats. Our work has been recognized by international organizations for its innovation and effectiveness.
