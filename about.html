About Us

Discover the story behind our drone charity.

Our History

Founded in 2020, Captain Elliot's Flying School began as a small initiative to teach safe drone operation to local communities. Over the years, we’ve grown into a leading charity using drones for humanitarian aid, environmental conservation, and education. Our journey started with a vision to bridge technology and compassion, and today, we’re making a global impact.

Our Team

Our dedicated team includes experienced drone pilots, engineers, educators, and conservationists. Led by Captain Elliot, a pioneer in drone technology, our team combines expertise with passion to drive our mission forward. We collaborate with schools, NGOs, and governments to maximize our reach and impact.

Our Values

We are committed to innovation, sustainability, education, and community empowerment. Every project we undertake reflects our core values of integrity, transparency, and making a difference. We believe in the power of drones to solve real-world problems and inspire future generations.
