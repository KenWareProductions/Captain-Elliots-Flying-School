Why Your Donation Matters

At Captain Elliot's Flying School, we rely on the generosity of supporters like you to continue our mission of using drones for humanitarian aid, environmental conservation, and educational outreach. Your contribution helps us deliver medical supplies to remote areas, monitor endangered species, and teach underprivileged children valuable skills in drone operation and technology.

How Your Donation Helps

  • Fund drone equipment and maintenance for disaster relief missions.
  • Support training programs for safe drone operation and the UK Drone Code.
  • Enable wildlife conservation efforts by monitoring habitats and species.
  • Provide educational resources for underprivileged schools.
